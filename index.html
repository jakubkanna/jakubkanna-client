Statement

With dedication for all of those who are brave enough to do things that most see as meaningless.

Z dedykacją dla wszystkich, którzy mają odwagę robić rzeczy, które większość uważa za bezsensowne.

Biogram

  • Childhood filled with sport education.
  • In 2022 studied at Academy of Fine Arts in Prague.
  • Holds BA in printmaking and MA in Intermedia at University of Arts in Poznan.
  • In 2024 finished fullstack developer course at TOP.
  • The most important realisations: Velo-art, Fight or Flight.
